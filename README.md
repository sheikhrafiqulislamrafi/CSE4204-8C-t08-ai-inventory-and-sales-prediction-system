# 🤖 AI Inventory & Sales Prediction System

> A smart web-based platform designed to help businesses manage inventory, track sales, and improve decision-making using Artificial Intelligence.
---
## 🚀 Project Description

The **AI Inventory & Sales Prediction System** is a smart web-based business management platform developed to solve common inventory and sales management problems faced by small and medium-sized businesses.

Traditional inventory systems mainly focus on basic product management and sales recording. However, they often lack intelligent forecasting capabilities and advanced analytics features. This project integrates **Artificial Intelligence** to analyze historical sales data, predict future product demand, and generate smart inventory recommendations.

### The system helps businesses:

- ✅ Reduce overstocking and stock shortages
- 📈 Analyze product performance
- 💹 Monitor revenue and sales trends
- 💡 Generate intelligent business insights
- 🗓️ Improve business planning and decision-making

The platform provides a **responsive and user-friendly interface** for both desktop and mobile users.
